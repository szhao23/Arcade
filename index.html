Welcome to the Arcade !


Where gamers are born ...

Here you will find three games: Tic-Tac-Toe, a turn-based two-player game in which the player goes against another player in a game of X's and O's to get three in a row to win! Snake, A game where you use the WASD keys to move Up, Left, Down, or Right and collect snake food and grow in size and survive as long as you can! Last but not least, Connect Four(Work-In-Progress), a game where you drop Red or Black pieces into the board and try your best to land four pieces in a row, whether it be diagonally, vertically, or horizontally!
